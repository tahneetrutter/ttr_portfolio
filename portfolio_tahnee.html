About Me

I am a Master's-qualified Architect based in Basel, with two years of professional experience at Diener & Diener Architekten and GRUNER&FRIENDS. My work focuses on delivering thoughtful, sustainable, and contextually-aware architectural solutions. I am passionate about leveraging design and technology to create spaces that are both beautiful and functional.

Philosophy

I believe in an architecture that responds sensitively to its climatic and urban context. My approach is rooted in urban continuity and spatial generosity, aiming to create buildings that not only serve their immediate purpose but also contribute positively to the public realm and the environment.
